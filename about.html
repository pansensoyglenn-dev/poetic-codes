Our Story

Poetic Codes began as a personal journey of Glenn Pansensoy, a web developer from Zamboanga Sibugay, Philippines, who discovered that the most effective way to teach programming concepts was through the lens of storytelling and real-world applications.

What started as a collection of Python tutorials has grown into a comprehensive learning platform that serves thousands of developers worldwide, helping them master Python OOP through clear explanations, practical examples, and engaging projects.
